Cast

Main Characters

The island works best when the town feels real enough to avoid after dark.

Primary signal

Lil’ Cabbage

Late-30s island outlaw with prophet energy, a weathered plaid jacket, a floppy cowboy hat, and no reliable plan.

Bad omen

Shithawk

A dog-sized seagull with glitchy PA-static wisdom. Could be divine. Could be corporate. Bad either way.

Municipal pressure

Barb Melvin

Civic hardass. Municipal fleece zipped to the throat. Runs local authority like a haunted HOA with a clipboard.

Side hustle operator

Trash Panda

Sharp-faced raccoon hustler in a shabby trench coat, forever one bad deal away from a breakthrough.

Currency evangelist

Crow

Leather-jacket systems obsessive pushing DustCoin and microcurrencies like salvation is one wallet app away.

Local myth

The Oil-Saint

An elder raccoon mystic with fryer soot markings and telepathic grease-riddles nobody asked for.
