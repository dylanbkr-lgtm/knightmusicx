Cast

Main Characters

The island works best when the town feels real enough to avoid after dark.

Human disaster

Lil’ Cabbage

Late-30s island menace in mud-caked denim and a floppy cowboy hat. Always feuding with the city, deeply poetic when mad.

Bylaw authority

Barb Melvin

Municipal fleece, clipboard grip of death, and zero patience. Mastermind of ChairGate and lead face of Crumby bureaucracy.

CAPA spokesperson

Ronnie

Nervous, earnest, and trapped in every non-animal crisis. Just wants peace. Will issue a press release for anything.

Fry strategist

Trash Panda

Fast-talking raccoon in a greasy trench coat. Speaks in schemes, bribes with wet fries, and may be blueprinting fry tunnels.

Conspiracy operator

Crow Gravyraven

Cracked leather jacket, silver chain, and a fry conspiracy board. Yells about FryCoin and thinks he is two steps ahead.

Fry thief

Daryl the Orca

Bucket-holding orca in aviators. Shows up in waters he has no business being in. Possibly part of a deep-fryer conspiracy.

Rap beef hazard

Shithawk

Dog-sized seagull with greasy feathers and wild yellow eyes. Loud, feral, camera-biting, and legally ungovernable.

Territorial threat

Harewood Harold

Menacing oversized peacock with grease-stained tail feathers. Believed to be in league with Daryl and known to hoard fries.

Silent menace

Buddy Bear

Giant quiet bear seen dragging deep fryers into the woods. Possibly cooking. Always watching. Never speaking.

Municipal escalation

Chair SWAT

Overfunded seating enforcement with tactical vests, Allen keys, and ominous Bluetooth speaker energy.

Tunnel network

Skidmark the Rat

Greasy rat with a zip-tie bandolier. Lives in storm drains, maps the tunnels, and works faster than anyone admits.

Backroad muscle

The Coyote Crew

Leather-vested coyotes on tiny bikes. Enforcers for Harold and Daryl’s syndicate. Bad news in formation.

Field Guide

The Lore

Crumby Island runs on recurring crises: civic pressure, fry economics, rogue wildlife, and warnings nobody takes seriously until the dock gets closed.

01

ChairGate

Lil’ Cabbage’s beloved chair gets confiscated for “safety violations.” Sit-ins, poetry threats, and civic panic follow.

02

The Fryspiracy

Deep fryers are disappearing. Daryl is involved. So are peacocks, coyotes, and probably somebody at City Hall.

03

Crumby vs. Zoo Crew

The animals are uniting, the city is panicking, and a turf war is brewing with burnt oil in the air.

04

Chair Amnesty Week

Barb calls it de-escalation. Cabbage calls it war. Everyone else just wants somewhere to sit.
