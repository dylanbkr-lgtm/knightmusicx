Incident Reports

Crumby Island runs on recurring crises: civic pressure, fry economics, rogue wildlife, and warnings nobody takes seriously until the dock gets closed.

Incident File #0001

ChairGate

Filed by: B. Melvin, Bylaw Enforcement

Status: ● Ongoing

Threat Level: Civic

Last updated: Following the third sit-in this month.

Lil’ Cabbage’s beloved chair was confiscated under Bylaw 7-14(b): Unsanctioned Seating. What followed: poetry threats, a sit-in, two CAPA press releases, and an emergency chair audit. The chair has not been returned.

Additional notes indicate the chair may possess heritage butt-groove value. This claim is not currently recognized by Crumby bylaw.

Incident File #0002

The Fryspiracy

Filed by: R. Ronnie, CAPA Spokesperson

Status: ● Escalating

Threat Level: Greaseborne

Last updated: After the deep fryer went missing from Dock 4.

Deep fryers are disappearing across the island. Daryl is involved. So are peacocks, coyotes, and probably somebody at City Hall, although City Hall has declined to confirm the existence of City Hall.

Read Full File ›

Witnesses report wet footprints, fryer baskets, and one suspicious bucket. CAPA recommends avoiding eye contact with any orca wearing aviators.

Incident File #0003

Crumby vs. Zoo Crew

Filed by: CAPA Front Desk

Status: ● Unresolved

Threat Level: Wildlife Civic

Last updated: When the coyotes arrived on tiny bikes.

The animals are uniting, the city is panicking, and a turf war is brewing with burnt oil in the air. Harewood Harold remains unhelpful and visually expensive.

Unverified reports connect Harold, Daryl, and the Coyote Crew through a diagram currently labelled “Fry-angle” in marker.
