About

Sylphina Veyra brings a glossy, ocean-lit pop signal into Knight Music X: tropical color, mall-mirror attitude, storm-bright hooks, and a sense of drama that knows exactly when to sparkle. Her world is bright on the surface, with darker water underneath.
